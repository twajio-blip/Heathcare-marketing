No, we typically operate on a flat retainer or performance-based fee structure rather than a percentage of ad spend.

Is your content reviewed by medical writers?

Yes, absolutely. Healthcare marketing requires extreme precision.

All clinical content, landing pages, and ad copy are drafted by specialized healthcare copywriters. Furthermore, content can be rigorously reviewed for medical accuracy and HIPAA compliance to ensure your practice builds unshakeable patient trust and avoids regulatory friction.
